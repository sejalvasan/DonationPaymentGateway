About Us

The Dog Bowl is a United States based, non-profit 501(c)(3) charity organization dedicated to supporting efficient and effective animal protection organizations in India. We strive to put our donor's generous contributions to work where they are needed the most.
The Dog Bowl develops close relationships with India's animal welfare NGOs. This ensures that all donations made to save India's animals are transparent and do the most good. In short, we provide donors confidence that their donations are put to the best use.
